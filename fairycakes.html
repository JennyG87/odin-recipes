Easy Peasy Fairy Cakes

Description

These easy peasy fairy cakes are fun to make with small children.

Add chocolate sprinkles, glace cherries or other toppings

Fairy Cake Recipe

  • 100 grams caster sugar
  • 100 grams of soft butter
  • 100 grams of self raising flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Icing Recipe

  • 200 grams very soft butter
  • 200 grams icing sugar

Steps

  1. Put the sugar and soft butter in a bowl and cream together. Sift in the flour gradually until mixture is smooth
  2. Add the eggs and vanilla extract and beat together
  3. Divide the mixture into 12 cup cake cases and put in the oven for 20 minutes at 180 degrees
  4. Mix the butter and the icing sugar together to make a creamy icing
  5. Once the cakes have cooled, spoon the icing sugar into an icing nozzle and apply to cakes
  6. Add toppings to the icing such as chocolate sprinkles or glace cherries
