Warming Vegetable Curry

Description

A deliciously warming vegetable curry, perfect for an autumn day.

Enjoy with basmati rice, naan breads and mango chutney

Spice Mix Ingredients

  • 2 tsp medium curry powder
  • 1 and 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds

Curry Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 large onion
  • 1 red chilli
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 1 large bell pepper
  • 4 cups potato
  • 2 cups cauliflower florets
  • 2 cups carrots
  • 2 handfuls of spinach
  • 2 tins of plum tomatoes
  • vegetable stock cube

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a large pot over a medium high heat. Add the garlic and onions and cook for 2 minutes
  2. Add the spice mix and cook for 1 minute until the spice is fragrant
  3. Add the cauliflower, bell pepper and potatoes and cook for 3 minutes
  4. Add the tinned tomatoes, plus one tin of water and the vegetable stock cube
  5. Bring to a simmer and cover.
  6. Cook for 40 minutes or until the vegetables are tender and the sauce has thickened
