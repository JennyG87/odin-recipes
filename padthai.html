Delicious Pad Thai

Description

Pad Thai is the number one selling Asian dish for good reason.

Packed with healthy vegetables and delicious flavours, this recipe will be a family favourite

Ingredients

  • 4 ounces of thick rice noodles
  • 2 shallots
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 packet stir fry vegetables
  • 500 grams of sliced pork tenderloin
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon garlic chilli sauce
  • 20 grams crushed peanuts
  • lime wedges to serve

Steps

  1. Prepare the rice noodles according to the package instructions
  2. Heat oil in a large wok and stirfy the pork until lightly brown. Remove the pork and set aside.
  3. Add the shallot and garlic to the wok and stirfy for one minute
  4. Add the pork and the stir fry vegetables and stir fry for two minutes
  5. Add the soy sauce, fish sauce and garlic chilli sauce and stir fry for 3 minutes or until the sauce is bubbling
  6. Add the noodles and stir fry for one minute
  7. Serve the Pad Thai in bowls with a sprinkling of the crushed peanuts and lime wedges
